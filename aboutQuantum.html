Smart Emergency Decision Support System for Palestine

Path2life is an innovative emergency decision support system designed to revolutionize ambulance dispatch and response efficiency in Palestine. Using advanced AI and quantum-inspired optimization algorithms, the system assists emergency operators by analyzing medical and contextual data in real-time to recommend the most appropriate ambulance and optimal route.

‚öõÔ∏è Quantum-Inspired Technology

Path2life uses cutting-edge quantum-inspired optimization algorithms that mimic the behavior of quantum computers. This allows the system to explore multiple route and ambulance combinations simultaneously, finding near-optimal solutions in milliseconds.

The system employs four key quantum concepts:

  • Superposition: Evaluates all possibilities at once
  • Quantum Annealing: Gradually converges to optimal solution
  • Quantum Tunneling: Escapes local minima to find global optimum
  • Entanglement: Considers correlations between risk, time, and resources
